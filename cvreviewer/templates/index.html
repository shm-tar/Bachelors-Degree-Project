How does it work?

Workflow

Upload your CV -> the system uses tokenization and anonymizes it -> other users check anonymous CVs and decide whether to give you the employment reference.

Upload a CV right away

Just quickly sign up if you want to have your CV anonymized or check basic features. Simple as that.

Track a CV

After upload, you can see your CV in the database and check/update/delete it.

Is it really anonymous?

Yes, others only see anonymized versions of CVs so they are not biased against someone. CVs won’t contain photos, personal info (gender, etc).
